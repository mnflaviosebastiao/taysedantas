About Tayse Dantas

Luxury Real Estate Advisor | Florida

With a solid executive background and a strategic perspective on real estate investment, Tayse Dantas serves as a trusted advisor in high-end residential and commercial real estate across Florida, assisting both U.S. and international clients with absolute discretion, excellence, and professionalism.

From Brazil to the United States, her journey has been shaped by executive leadership, high-level relationships, and a deep understanding of wealth, value, and positioning. This experience translates into a highly selective approach to the American real estate market, with a focus on exclusive opportunities, off-market assets, and carefully curated projects.

Tayse advises buyers and investors throughout Florida, providing direct access to developers, new construction, vacation residences, income-generating properties, and strategic assets, always through a personalized approach centered on capital preservation and long-term growth.

Recognized for her refined eye, clear communication, and ethical guidance throughout every transaction, she believes real estate goes beyond property — it is a decision of lifestyle, legacy, and long-term vision.
